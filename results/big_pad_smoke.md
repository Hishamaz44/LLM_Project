## t01 — Should social media platforms be legally liable for user-generated misinformation?

### Round 1 — Opening statements

**~60 words**

**For Debater**

Social media platforms are not neutral; their algorithms amplify misinformation for profit, causing real harm. The COVID-19 infodemic proved deadly—falsehoods on Facebook alone led to vaccine hesitancy and preventable deaths. With great power comes responsibility. Legal liability would force platforms to prioritize public safety over engagement, just as car manufacturers must for defective vehicles.

**Against Debater**

The real antidote to bad speech is more speech and media literacy, not making digital town squares legally accountable for billions of daily posts.
